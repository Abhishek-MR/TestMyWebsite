Let's grow together

Gems ‘n’ Pearls is an educational hub established in the year 2011 facilitating empowerment of individuals across age groups starting as early as 1.8years to any age through various educational segments. Under the brand name Gems ‘n’ Pearls we run a Preschool in Ajmera, the Area franchisee of Helen O’Grady International Edudrama Academy in Pimpri- Chinchwad , Franchisee for Chrysaalis i-Maths “Intelligent Maths” at Ajmera and Pradhikaran, franchisee for Early Child Care Education – preschool teacher training program, Corporate Training, and Spoken English for adults. Gems ‘n’ Pearls Academy is moving into its 10 th Academic year with 2020-21

Our philosophy

Research states 90% of the brain develops by the age of 5 years and a child shapes his or her beliefs, values and life in these primitive years. At Gems ’n’ Pearls preschool, we provide a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for all children. We believe in allowing children to express themselves through language, art, creativity and physical activity, by providing an environment that is challenging and fun. We trust experiential learning is deemed to empower children and hence facilitating an environment that welcomes every child into its system and allows them to explore, have fun and learn or imbibe life skills is our philosophy.
